Amber rated it:

Love this Series!
My daughter is a huge fan of all the "Old Lady" books by Lucille Colandro. She reads them all the time, and covets them above all other books in her collection. She is not a strong reader yet, but she has read them so many times that she has them memorized. This is the 1st one we bought for our collection and it is just as funny and cute as the others. She always wants to see what the old lady will burp out at the end! She enjoys reading these to her little brother, who gets just as excited as her with every turn of the page!

Classroom Uses: Reading Along
Great for: Belly Laughing
