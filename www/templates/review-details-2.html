Pete the Cat: I Love My White Shoes

Eric Litwin
Description

Pete is one cool cat! In this splashy picture-book companion to the rock-and-roll kids' song, Pete doesn't let anything keep him from movin' and groovin' and singin' his song.

Featured Review

Hannabannana rated it:

White Shoes
We love this book so much, we can't keep ourselves from "singing the song"... One day I was rushing to get to work and shuffling through the fridge I accidentally spilled a whole bowl of pickled beets onto the floor and it splattered and got on my new white sneakers! I took a deep breath and began to laugh and started to sing..."I love my red shoes...." my 2 1/2 year old started singing with me!

Classroom Uses: Reading Aloud
Great for: Giving as a Gift, Belly Laughing
